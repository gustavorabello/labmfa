Eduardo Vitral Freigedo Rodrigues was an M.Sc. student at `UERJ`_/`Gesar`_,
supervised by `Prof. Gustavo R. Anjos`_ and co-supervised by `Prof. José
Pontes`_. The project, Nano-Patterning of Surfaces by Ion Sputtering –
Numerical Study of the Anisotropic Damped Kuramoto–Sivashinsky Equation, is
associated with LabMFA research in computational mechanics, finite difference
methods, fluid dynamics, heat transfer, and numerical methods.
