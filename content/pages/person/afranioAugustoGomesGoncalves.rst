Afrânio Augusto Gomes Gonçalves is an M.Sc. student in Mechanical Engineering
at `UFRJ`_/`Coppe`_ under the supervision of `Prof. Gustavo R. Anjos`_. His
research, Finite Element Analysis of Incompressible Flows in Complex
Geometries: A Hydrodynamic Study Using Triangular and Quadrilateral Meshes, is
developed within LabMFA and focuses on computational mechanics, fluid dynamics,
heat transfer, and numerical methods. He is a Mechanical Engineer with
postgraduate training in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science and
professional experience in offshore operations, subsea robotics, maintenance
planning, and data-driven engineering projects. His interests include
computational fluid dynamics, scientific computing, machine learning, and
engineering analytics.
