I am an undergraduate exchange student from `Centrale Méditerranée`_ currently
studying at UFRJ, with interests in artificial intelligence, finite element
methods, and scientific computing applied to fluid mechanics. I develop
computational and programming skills using Python and FreeFEM++ for numerical
simulations and fluid dynamics applications. Currently, I am working at LABMFA
under the supervision of `Prof. Gustavo R. Anjos`_. My academic interests focus
on numerical methods, scientific computing, and the application of AI
techniques to engineering and physics simulations.
