I am a Mechanical Engineering undergraduate student at UFRJ and currently serve as the Undergraduate Researcher
for a project funded by the National Agency of Petroleum (ANP). Under the guidance of `Prof. Gustavo R. Anjos`_,
I am developing a custom Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) solver in Python based on the Finite Element Method (FEM).
My research focuses on evaluating the multiphase flow of air and B100 biodiesel within a combustion chamber,
specifically analyzing the spray cone angle, penetration, and other injection parameters to optimize biofuel
implementation in modern internal combustion engines.
