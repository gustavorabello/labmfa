Jose Lages da Silva Neto is a D.Sc. student in Mechanical Engineering at Rio de
Janeiro State Univeristy `UERJ`_. His research focuses on heat transfer in
extended surfaces, with emphasis on mathematical modeling, numerical methods,
and nonlinear thermal radiation problems. His current work involves the
analysis of conduction–radiation heat transfer using finite element methods and
variational formulations. He is associated with LabMFA research in
computational mechanics, fluid dynamics, heat transfer, and numerical methods
under the supervision of `Prof. Eduardo Dias Correa`_, Prof. Rogério Saldanha
and `Prof. Gustavo R. Anjos`_.
