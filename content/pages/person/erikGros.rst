Erik Gros was a D.Sc. student at `EPFL`_ under the supervision of `Prof.
Gustavo R. Anjos`_ and `Prof. John R. Thome`_. The project, *Numerical Modelling of
Two-Phase Flow with Moving Boundary-Fitted Meshes*, is associated with LabMFA
research in computational mechanics, fluid dynamics, heat transfer, and
numerical methods.
